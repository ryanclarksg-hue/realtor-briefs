The Challenge

Managing the season
at full scale.

During peak season you're managing 15–18 people across multiple job sites spanning Fort Walton Beach, Niceville, Destin, and Pensacola — all at once. Keeping track of everything relies on phone calls, memory, and trust.

📞
No Proof of Presence
When a crew member says they're on site, there's no automatic record of exactly where they were and when. That's a liability if a client or municipality ever questions it.
📋
Manual Timekeeping
Paper timesheets, text messages, or verbal reports are slow, error-prone, and nearly impossible to audit. Payroll becomes a guessing game at the end of the week.
🗺️
No Big Picture View
With crews spread across the coast, there's no single place to see who is where, what's been completed, and what still needs attention — right now, today.

The Employee
Mobile App

Your crew opens a simple app on any smartphone — no download required, no App Store, no setup. It works right in the phone's browser. One tap to clock in. One tap to clock out. That's it.

  • GPS coordinates are captured automatically the moment they tap Clock In — logged with a timestamp and tied to their name
  • Crew can take and submit job site photos directly from the app — before, during, and after
  • A simple counter lets them log how many trees they wrapped that day
  • Works on any iPhone or Android with no installation required
  • Each crew member has their own secure login so records are always accurate

The Operations
Dashboard

Your operations manager opens a private website on any laptop or tablet. In seconds, they can see exactly who is clocked in, where they are, and what they've accomplished — without making a single phone call.

  • Live crew board showing every employee's status, current location, and hours logged today
  • Automatic alerts when a crew member hasn't clocked in by their expected start time
  • All photos submitted from the field appear here, tagged by location and employee
  • Running totals for trees wrapped per site, per crew member, and for the entire season
  • One-click payroll export to CSV at the end of each week — clean and ready for processing

The Live
Operations Map

Every clock-in plants a pin on the map automatically. By the end of the season, you'll have a complete visual record of everywhere your team has worked — from Niceville to Pensacola Beach.

  • Every active job site appears as a live pin on a real map of the Emerald Coast
  • Click any pin to see the full history — who worked there, hours logged, photos, trees wrapped
  • Sites stay on the map all season, building a permanent record of your operational footprint
  • Completed sites are marked differently so the ops manager knows what's active vs. done
  • The map becomes a powerful planning tool — see where you've been and what's coming next

Access & Connectivity

Two portals.
One connected system.

Everything runs through secure, private web addresses that only your team can access. Nothing to install. No servers to manage. It all lives in the cloud and works from any device with a browser.

📱
Crew Access
Every crew member gets a simple link to bookmark on their phone. When they open it, they're taken directly to their clock-in screen. No passwords to remember after the first login, no app to update, no confusion.

Works on any iPhone or Android, any carrier, even on slower connections. Designed for the field — big buttons, minimal steps, impossible to mess up.

🖥️
Manager Access
Your operations manager gets a separate, password-protected URL that opens the full dashboard. Works on any laptop, tablet, or desktop — no software to install. They can check in from the office, from home, or from the road.

The dashboard updates in real time — the moment a crew member clocks in or submits a photo, it appears instantly. You're never more than a glance away from knowing exactly what's happening.
