Lead Intelligence System

28,112 Motivated Sellers. Already Identified.

We've already built this for Okaloosa County. The absentee owner database exists. It's scored. It's ranked. The question is just putting it in front of the right agent — one who can actually serve those sellers better than anyone else in the market.

🎯 The Okaloosa Absentee Owner System
Already Built

Using the Florida Department of Revenue's 2025 NAL Tax Roll — public data — we've already identified 28,112 Okaloosa County property owners whose mailing address differs from their property address. Every record is scored by likelihood to sell. The HOT tier alone has 1,236 records. Jim's 1–2% commission model is a compelling reason for every one of them to call him instead of a 6% agent.

  • 28,112 absentee owner records — names, property addresses, mailing addresses, property values
  • Lead scoring system — 100-point model · 1,236 HOT leads · 11,113 WARM leads
  • Broken down by city — Destin 9,317 · Fort Walton Beach 8,751 · Crestview 4,466
  • 329 estate/heir situations flagged — Jim's stated specialty · Highest motivation to sell
  • Commission savings calculator — show each lead their exact dollar savings with Jim vs. 6%
🏚 The Distressed Property Monitor
Concept · Buildable

Okaloosa County Clerk of Courts publishes lis pendens filings weekly — the formal start of foreclosure. Cross-referencing these against high-equity properties in our existing database surfaces the most motivated sellers in the county. Jim's estate sale specialty makes him the perfect agent for these conversations.

  • Weekly lis pendens pull · New filings only · Cross-referenced against our absentee owner data
  • Flag properties with estimated equity over $75K — real money at stake for the seller
  • Generate a brief weekly alert — who they are, property value, equity estimate, how to reach them
  • Jim's pitch: "You have equity to protect. Let's sell before the bank takes it — at 2%, not 6%."
💰 The Commission Savings Mailer
Concept · Buildable

Jim's biggest competitive advantage is financial — and most homeowners don't know what they're leaving on the table. A personalized mailer showing each absentee owner their exact commission savings could be the most effective direct mail campaign in Okaloosa County history.

  • Pull property value from tax roll for each lead
  • Calculate 6% traditional commission vs. Jim's 2% — show the exact dollar difference
  • Personalized letter: "Your property at [address] is worth approximately $[value]. At 6%, you'd pay $[X]. With Uber Realty, you pay $[Y]. That's $[savings] you keep."
  • Output as print-ready mail merge — ready for Jim's mail vendor · Targeted to HOT leads first

The Listing Machine

Every Appointment. Already Prepared.

Jim runs a lean, one-man operation. Every hour he spends on preparation, paperwork, or administrative tasks is an hour he's not talking to a client. The listing machine concept is designed specifically for how Jim operates — fast, personal, and data-driven.

📋 Pre-Listing Intelligence Package
Concept · Buildable

The moment Jim confirms a listing appointment, a system could automatically pull everything he needs — property record, ownership history, comparable sales, neighborhood trends — and deliver a one-page brief to his phone before he walks in the door.

  • Pull Okaloosa County property record — assessed value, year built, square footage, ownership history
  • Run automated comp analysis — recent sales within 0.5 miles, last 90 days, price per sqft
  • Calculate estimated equity — current value minus estimated remaining mortgage
  • Flag any lis pendens, tax delinquency, or other public filings on the property
  • One-page brief — delivered to Jim's email before the appointment
📊 Personalized Listing Presentation Builder
Concept · Buildable

Jim already publishes sophisticated MLS market reports. A presentation builder could take that same analytical intelligence and turn it into a customized, property-specific presentation — automatically — for every single listing appointment.

  • Auto-populate seller name, property address, and neighborhood into Uber Realty template
  • Insert local comps, days-on-market trends, and price-per-sqft data
  • Show the commission savings calculation — front and center — with their specific numbers
  • Output as branded PDF · Jim walks in with something nobody else brings

Transaction Systems

Under Contract to Close. Nothing Missed.

Jim personally handles every transaction — which means every deadline, every document, every follow-up lives in his head. A lightweight system that tracks the timeline and handles the routine work frees Jim to focus on what only he can do: talking to clients and solving problems.

⏱ Transaction Timeline Tracker
Concept

Automatically maps every critical date the moment a contract is executed. Sends Jim proactive alerts before each deadline — inspection, financing, appraisal, walk-through, closing.

  • Extract all key dates from executed contract
  • Build a countdown to every milestone
  • Alert 5 days, 2 days, and day-of each deadline
  • Daily one-line status: "3 active deals — 1 deadline in 2 days"
📄 Pin-to-Close Generator
Concept

At closing, a clean branded summary document — automatically generated from transaction data — recapping the entire deal. Professional, consistent, effortless.

  • Pull transaction data automatically
  • Generate formatted closing summary
  • Include all parties, dates, final financials
  • Output as Uber Realty branded PDF
