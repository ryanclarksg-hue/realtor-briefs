02
Lead Generation

A Proactive Lead Intelligence System

Most agents wait for leads to come to them. What if Shannon's team could identify motivated sellers in Hamilton County before they ever list — using only public data, smart analysis, and the right outreach strategy?

🎯 The Absentee Owner Machine
Concept · Buildable

Indiana publishes public property records for every county. By comparing owner mailing addresses to property addresses, it's possible to identify homeowners who don't live at their property — a strong signal of motivated sellers. This system could deliver a fresh, scored lead list to Shannon every month.

  • Pull Hamilton County public property records monthly — owner names, addresses, assessed values
  • Identify absentee owners — mailing address differs from property address
  • Score each lead — long-term ownership, out-of-state, high equity, estate situations, property value
  • Deliver a ranked HOT list — Shannon gets the top 50–100 most motivated sellers each month
  • Generate personalized outreach letters — ready to mail, in her voice
🏚 The Distressed Property Monitor
Concept · Buildable

Hamilton County Clerk of Courts publishes lis pendens filings — the legal notice that foreclosure has begun — as public record. Cross-referencing these against high-equity properties could surface sellers who desperately need help before the bank takes their home.

  • Monitor Hamilton County lis pendens filings weekly — new filings only
  • Cross-reference against properties with estimated equity over $100K
  • Flag owners who are also absentee — highest motivation to sell quickly
  • Alert Shannon with a brief summary — who they are, what the property is worth, how to reach them
🔄 The Sphere & Referral Re-Engagement System
Concept · Buildable

Shannon has likely closed 800–1,400+ transactions over 18 years. That's an enormous sphere of past clients. A systematic re-engagement program — timed around ownership anniversaries, market conditions, and equity milestones — could generate consistent warm listing leads from people who already trust her.

  • Pull past client list from Lofty CRM — organize by close date and neighborhood
  • Calculate ownership anniversary dates — trigger outreach at 3, 5, 7, and 10 year marks
  • Monitor market value of past client homes — alert when equity has grown significantly
  • Draft personalized anniversary check-in emails — in Shannon's voice — for her review and send
03
The Listing Machine

From Appointment to Active Listing

When Shannon gets a listing appointment, she shouldn't be thinking about paperwork, market reports, or presentation materials. Every one of those things should already be done — waiting for her when she walks in the door.

📋 Pre-Listing Intelligence Package
Concept · Buildable

The moment a listing appointment is confirmed, a system could automatically begin assembling everything Shannon needs — so she walks in prepared, informed, and impressive. Not just a CMA. A complete intelligence package on the property, the seller, and the market.

  • Pull public property record — ownership history, assessed value, year built, square footage, tax history
  • Run automated CMA — recent comparable sales within 0.5 miles, last 90 days
  • Analyze the neighborhood — average days on market, price per sqft trends, recent list-to-sale ratios
  • Research the seller — ownership duration, equity estimate, any public filings
  • Draft a one-page property summary — ready for Shannon to review before the appointment
  • Flag anything unusual — recent price reductions nearby, competing active listings, market softening
🏆 Listing Presentation Builder
Concept · Buildable

A dynamic listing presentation — automatically customized for each property and seller — that Shannon can walk in with every time. Not a generic template. A presentation that already has the seller's address, the comparable sales, Shannon's relevant stats for that neighborhood, and a clear pricing recommendation.

  • Auto-populate seller name, property address, and listing date into the presentation template
  • Insert neighborhood-specific comps and market trend data
  • Pull Shannon's personal stats for that zip code — her avg days on market, list-to-sale ratio
  • Generate a recommended pricing range with supporting data
  • Output as a polished PDF — branded, professional, ready to present
📸 Listing Launch Checklist
Concept · Buildable

Every listing has the same checklist. Photos, staging, MLS input, social posts, open house scheduling, sign orders, lockbox setup. A system that tracks every step, assigns owners, and alerts the team when something is pending — so nothing falls through the cracks.

  • Auto-generate the full launch checklist the moment a listing is signed
  • Assign tasks to team members with deadlines
  • Draft MLS description — for Shannon's review and edit
  • Draft 3 social media posts — coming soon, just listed, open house
  • Flag any missing items 48 hours before go-live date

⏱ Transaction Timeline Tracker
Concept · Buildable

Every transaction has the same critical dates — inspection window, financing contingency, appraisal deadline, walk-through, closing. A system that automatically maps these dates the moment a contract is signed and alerts the team before every single one.

  • Extract all key dates from the executed contract automatically
  • Build a countdown timeline — days remaining on each milestone
  • Send proactive alerts 5 days, 2 days, and day-of each deadline

  • Generate a daily transaction status summary — one clean view of every active deal
