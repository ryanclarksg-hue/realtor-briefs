Lead Intelligence

Santa Rosa County's Hidden Seller Pool

Navarre sits in Santa Rosa County — a fast-growing market with strong military relocation activity, new construction demand, and a significant absentee owner population. The same public data system we built for Okaloosa County can be replicated here.

🎯 Santa Rosa County Absentee Owner System
Concept · Buildable

Florida's public property data covers all 67 counties — including Santa Rosa. The same absentee owner identification and lead scoring system we built for Okaloosa can be rebuilt for Shelby's exact market. Navarre has a significant vacation home and investment property population given its proximity to the beach.

  • Pull Santa Rosa County 2025 NAL Tax Roll — same methodology as Okaloosa County
  • Identify absentee owners in Navarre, Navarre Beach, Gulf Breeze, and surrounding areas
  • Score each lead — beach proximity, long-term ownership, out-of-state owners, property value
  • Flag new construction properties specifically — Shelby's sweet spot, buyers and sellers
  • Deliver monthly ranked HOT list — Shelby's ready-to-contact motivated seller pipeline
🪖 Military Relocation Intelligence
Concept · Buildable

Navarre is directly adjacent to Hurlburt Field and Eglin Air Force Base — two of the largest military installations in the country. Military families relocate on 2–3 year cycles, creating a constant flow of buyers and sellers. A system targeting military homeowners approaching their typical PCS window could be a significant lead source unique to Shelby's market.

  • Identify properties in military-adjacent zip codes purchased 2–3 years ago
  • Cross-reference with absentee owner data — military who've already PCS'd and retained property
  • Alert Shelby to properties likely to hit the market in the next 6 months
  • Outreach messaging tailored to military families — "When you PCS, we make it easy"

Operations Systems

From Appointment to Closing — Effortlessly

The same listing machine and transaction systems we've designed for other agents apply directly to Shelby's business — with one important addition: new construction transactions have unique timelines, builder communication requirements, and milestone tracking that a standard system doesn't cover.

🏗️ New Construction Transaction Tracker
Concept

New construction transactions are longer and more complex than resale — build timelines, draw schedules, inspection phases, and builder communication all need tracking. A system built specifically for new construction could be Shelby's biggest operational win.

  • Track build milestone timeline from contract to close
  • Builder communication log — what was promised, when
  • Phase inspection reminders — foundation, framing, final
  • Client update emails drafted automatically at each milestone
📋 Pre-Listing Intelligence Package
Concept

Every listing appointment automatically triggers a full property brief — comp analysis, ownership history, neighborhood trends, and estimated equity. Shelby walks in knowing more about the seller's property than they do.

  • Pull Santa Rosa County property record automatically
  • Comparable sales within 0.5 miles · Last 90 days
  • New construction activity nearby — listings, pricing, competition
  • One-page brief delivered before the appointment
