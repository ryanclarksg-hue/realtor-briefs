What Public Data Might Reveal

The Intelligence That Could Exist

Indiana — like Florida — publishes substantial public property data. With the right analysis, that data could potentially reveal patterns, opportunities, and lead signals that most agents never see. The following represents what might be possible — not what currently exists.

Hypothetical Lead Intelligence

The Hamilton County Absentee Owner Opportunity

Using Indiana's public property records, it may be possible to identify homeowners in Hamilton County whose mailing address differs from their property address — a strong indicator of absentee ownership, rental properties, or vacation homes. In comparable Florida counties, this analysis has revealed 28,000+ such properties. Hamilton County's high property values could make this an even more valuable lead source — though the exact numbers would require verification against Indiana's public data.

A Suggested Path Forward

How This Could Come to Life

If Shannon were interested in exploring what's possible, here is a suggested phased approach — starting with what's simple and free, building toward what's more sophisticated over time.

Phase One · Start Here
Intelligence Delivery — Show What's Possible
Pull Hamilton County public property data. Build a scored absentee owner list for her exact market. Present a data-driven picture of the lead landscape she's operating in — using only public records. No software required. No cost. Just insight.
Phase Two · Quick Win
Lofty CRM Setup & Optimization
Shannon is already paying for Lofty — a powerful AI-enabled CRM. Setting it up properly (Smart Plans, pipelines, lead tagging, AI Copilot) could recapture significant daily time without building anything new. This is the fastest ROI available.
Phase Three · Build
Lead Intelligence System — Hamilton County
Build the absentee owner scoring system for her market. Identify the highest-probability sellers in Hamilton County using public data. Deliver a monthly ranked list she can actually act on. This is the core of the intelligence opportunity.
Phase Four · Expand
Distressed Property Monitoring
Begin monitoring Hamilton County lis pendens filings and tax delinquency records. Cross-reference against high-equity properties. Alert Shannon to motivated sellers before they hit public channels. This is the market intelligence edge most agents don't have.
Phase Five · Long-Term Vision
Full AI Operations Layer
Email intelligence, transaction coordinator agent, social content drafting, pipeline dashboard. The full vision — an AI-powered operations layer that runs quietly in the background while Shannon focuses on what she does best: relationships and closings. This phase requires significant planning, compliance review, and integration work.
