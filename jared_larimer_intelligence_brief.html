Deal Sourcing Systems

A Constant Pipeline of Motivated Sellers

The difference between a good month and a great month for Jared is deal flow. The following systems are designed to keep a steady stream of scored, motivated sellers in front of him — automatically — so he's always working the best available opportunities.

🚨 Weekly Pre-Foreclosure Alert System
Highest Priority · Buildable

Okaloosa County Clerk of Courts publishes lis pendens filings weekly — the formal start of foreclosure. These are Jared's absolute best leads. The homeowner has a few months before the bank takes everything. A cash buyer who calls them first — with a fair offer and a fast close — is exactly what they need. Every week, a new batch of these hits the public record.

  • Pull new Okaloosa County lis pendens filings every Monday morning
  • Cross-reference against our existing database — get owner name, mailing address, property value, equity estimate
  • Score each filing — how much equity, how long owned, absentee or occupied
  • Generate a weekly alert: "3 new pre-foreclosure properties this week — here's who to call first"
  • Draft personalized outreach letter for each — "I can close in 7 days. You keep your equity."
🎯 Monthly Distressed Seller HOT List
Data Already Built

Our existing 28,112 record Okaloosa County database is already scored and ranked. Every month, we can deliver Jared the top 50 most motivated potential sellers — filtered specifically for the situations his business was built to solve. Estate heirs, tired landlords, out-of-state owners, tax delinquents.

  • Filter the database for Jared's specific deal criteria — distress signals, equity, property type
  • Rank the top 50 by deal score — highest motivation first
  • Include owner name, property address, mailing address, estimated equity, distress type
  • Flag multi-family properties specifically — Jared's stated growth target
  • Refresh monthly — new owners in, closed deals out · Always fresh pipeline
🏢 Multi-Family & Landlord Targeting
Concept · Buildable

Jared actively posts about buying duplexes and multi-family properties up to 60 units. Our database already tags multi-family absentee owners. A targeted system specifically hunting tired landlords — people managing rentals from out of state — could be his most valuable deal source.

  • Filter database for DOR codes 3 and 8 — multi-family residential properties
  • Cross-reference with out-of-state owners — remote landlord = highest fatigue
  • Flag those owned 5+ years with no recent sale activity
  • Personalized outreach: "Managing rentals from out of state is exhausting. I'll buy them all — cash, as-is."
📬 Automated Direct Mail Campaign
Concept · Buildable

Jared already does direct mail — it's standard for cash buyers. What we add is intelligence. Instead of blasting generic postcards to random neighborhoods, every mailer is targeted to a scored lead with a personalized message based on their specific situation — pre-foreclosure, inherited, absentee landlord.

  • Pull monthly HOT list · Sort by distress type
  • Generate situation-specific letter — pre-foreclosure gets different message than estate heir
  • Include estimated equity: "Your property at [address] may have $[X] in equity. Let's protect it."
  • Output print-ready mail merge — JNL Solutions branded · Ready for mail vendor
  • Track which mailer types convert to calls over time · Optimize monthly

Deal Operations

From First Contact to Closed Deal

Jared closes fast — 7 to 14 days is his brand promise. That speed requires clean systems. The following concepts are designed to keep every deal moving and nothing slipping through the cracks.

⚡ Deal Pipeline Tracker
Concept

Every active deal tracked from first contact to close. Stages, deadlines, action items — all in one view. Nothing falls through the cracks when you're running 5 deals at once.

  • Lead → Contacted → Offer Made → Under Contract → Closed
  • Days since last contact flagged automatically
  • Closing deadline countdown on every active deal
  • Daily one-line digest: "2 under contract · 1 offer pending · 8 new leads"
📊 Deal Analyzer
Concept

When Jared gets a new lead, a system could automatically pull the property data — assessed value, ownership history, comparable sales, neighborhood trends — and calculate a quick deal analysis: ARV, estimated repair budget, potential profit margin.

  • Pull Okaloosa County property record automatically
  • Run comparable sold analysis — ARV estimate
  • Calculate rough deal metrics — buy price target, estimated margin
  • One-page deal sheet — ready before Jared calls the seller
